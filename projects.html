My Projects

Fridge Daddy

Fridge Daddy was a project worked on in CISC 474, where myself
and a group of students made a food and recipe website. I personally
had a lot of work in the back-end of logins and profiles. We accomplished
this using firebase. On the right, you can view the github page for this project.



Plate Builder

The plate builder was my capstone project while at UD. Myself and six
other students worked together with Frontier Scientific to work on a plate
builder for putting together specific compunds and keeping track of them all.
I was involved in the import and export functionallity of all the data, but
also had my hand in helping with the general website design and the back-end logins.
On the right you can find the github page for this project. It is still
being worked on by our group now.
